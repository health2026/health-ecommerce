# Facebook Ads Strategy & Video Scripts

This document outlines the marketing strategy and video scripts for the health e-commerce products. Each product has 3 distinct video concepts: **Problem/Solution**, **Lifestyle/Aesthetic**, and **Social Proof/Review**. All scripts are written in English for maximum conversion on global platforms.

---

## 1. Smart Baby Thermometer PRO
**Goal:** Build trust and peace of mind for parents.

### Video 1: The Worried Parent (Problem/Solution)
*   **Hook (0-3s):** 
*   **Body (3-12s):** Show tA mother waking up at 3 AM looking exhausted and touching her baby's forehead. Text overlay: "Tired of waking up every hour to check your baby's fever?"he Smart Thermometer being applied gently. Cut to a smartphone screen showing a real-time graph. "Get 24/7 continuous monitoring without waking them up."
*   **CTA (12-15s):** "Protect your baby's sleep. Get yours now!"

### Video 2: Tech-Savvy Parenting (Aesthetic/Features)
*   **Hook (0-3s):** Fast cuts of the sleek thermometer device, the app interface, and the Xiaomi logo. Text: "The Smartest Thermometer in 2026."
*   **Body (3-12s):** Demonstrate the high-temp alarm. "Instantly alerts your phone if a fever starts." Show medical-grade adhesive.
*   **CTA (12-15s):** "Limited Time Offer: Get 30% OFF Today!"

## 2. Herbal Detox Foot Soak
**Goal:** Create a desire for relaxation and "invisible" health benefits.

### Video 1: The Swolen Feet Relief (Problem/Solution)
*   **Hook (0-3s):** Close up of tired, swollen feet after a long day of work. Text: "Feet hurt? Body feels heavy?"
*   **Body (3-12s):** Show the herbal bag dropping into warm water, the water turning dark green/brown. Show a person relaxing with a book. "Drain toxins and reduce swelling naturally."
*   **CTA (12-15s):** "Restart your body tonight. Shop the Detox."

### Video 2: Pure Ingredients (Aesthetic)
*   **Hook (0-3s):** Beautiful macro shots of Ginger, Saffron, and Mugwort. Soft, calming music.
*   **Body (3-12s):** Slow-motion pouring of water. Text: "100% Organic. NO chemicals. Just results."
*   **CTA (12-15s):** "Experience the natural spa. Buy now."

## 3. Pro Honeycomb Knee Pads
**Goal:** Target athletes and active people with protection and performance.

### Video 1: The "Stay in the Game" (Problem/Solution)
*   **Hook (0-3s):** A basketball player falling on their knees. Ouch! Text: "Don't let a fall end your season."
*   **Body (3-12s):** Show the Honeycomb technology absorbing an impact. Slow motion jump and landing. "Impact protection where you need it most."
*   **CTA (12-15s):** "Play without fear. Order your pair."

### Video 2: Performance & Comfort (Feature)
*   **Hook (0-3s):** Person putting on the pads. They look sleek and professional.
*   **Body (3-12s):** Close up of the breathable fabric. Person running/squatting without the pads moving. "Stay cool. Stay dry. Never slips."
*   **CTA (12-15s):** "The Athlete's Choice. 50% Off Bundle!"
